About Scholar SA

Founded in 2022, Scholar SA is a nonprofit dedicated to supporting children with uniforms, school shoes, extra classes, sports programs, and feminine hygiene products.

Our Mission

We empower children by providing the resources they need to thrive academically, discover their talents, and build a better future.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every child, regardless of background, has access to education, sports, and personal development opportunities.

Our Impact

Since 2022, we've provided school uniforms to 500+ children, helped 200 students improve academically, and supported 100+ young athletes through sports programs.
